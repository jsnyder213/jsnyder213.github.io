2016 - Current: Operations Manager

My current job is the Operations Manager with the Shelden clinical simulation center at the University of Missouri. I have been with the simulation center for going on 6 years, starting at a simulation technican and advanceing myself into my current role as Operations manager. As the operations manager I am involved in the general day to day functionality of the center, this includes (but is not limited to)

Job Duties:

  • Project Management
  • Adminstration of a Learning Management system
  • Tech support

In this position I also manage a team of 3 people, as well as assit the director in the management of the department as a whole. Aside from the various responsibilities I have, that challenge me daily, one of the best aspects of this job is the technical work I do. I am responsibile for the upkeep and maintance of the center's high-fidelty simulators. These robots are used to help the center accomplish the mission of medical education in a safe and responbile learning environment.

2009 - 2016: State of Missouri

For years I worked at the state in various helpdesk/technical positions, going from an Information Techologist moving up to an Information Technology Specialist. I ended my career with the state (before moving to the University) at the Department of Conservation. Throughout the years with the state my responsibilities ranged from: telecommunications, statewide wireless contracts and services, IT helpdesk, implementation of statewide phones systems, software development and packaging, network admistration, and many others.

The experience gained from this time with the state gave me the confidence to move to my current position with the University, with a lot of the past experience being directly tied to some of my current responisbilities.
