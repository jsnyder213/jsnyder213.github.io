2016 - Current: Operations Manager

My current job is the Operations Manager with the Shelden clinical simulation center at the University of Missouri. I have been with the simulation center for going on 6 years, starting at a simulation technican and advanceing myself into my current role as Operations manager. As the operations manager I am involved in the general day to day functionality of the center, this includes (but is not limited to): project management, administration of our learning management system, technical support, software system management and deployment, managin audio/visual installation and ensuring the department meets the educational expectations of Universtiy.

In this position I also manage a team of 3 people, as well as assit the director in the management of the department as a whole. Aside from the various responsibilities I have, that challenge me daily, one of the best aspects of this job is the technical work I do. I am responsibile for the upkeep and maintance of the center's high-fidelty simulators. These robots are used to help the center accomplish the mission of medical education in a safe and responbile learning environment.
