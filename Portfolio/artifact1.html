Artifact 1:

Artifact Descripition:

This project was a group project for the Intro to VR course. My partner and I were tasked with creating a Virtual Reality Learning Experience (VRLE). with at least 4 rooms. Our project subject was focused on building a VRLE that could be used with special needs learners, specifically geared toward social awareness actvities. My partner and I took on a split role with us both contributing equal amoutns of responsibility. Those duties included: Layout and desing,3D modeling, content write-up, discussion postings, and the eventual group presentation to the class.

Artifact Refleection:

In my opinion, the content of this project is a wonderful example of how technology, specifically VR, can be used to help reach and educate students (as well as parents) who may struggle with social situations. The concept of this project was to create a world that these students along with teachers and parents can interact in socially charged environments. As an example, we built a mock up of a "party room" or a large classroom. Everyday settings that may present over stimulation for these learners allowing them to overcome anxeities and learn different ways to react and control the situation for themselves.
