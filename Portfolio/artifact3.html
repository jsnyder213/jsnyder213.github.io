Artifact 3:

Objective 1.1: Students are able to design/develop learning performance opportunities and systems including direct instruction, constructivist learning, collaborative work, and performance support.

Objective 1.3: Students are able to conduct project management activities for both design/development, and implementation of learning opportunities and systems.

Artifact Description:

This artifact was a group project for the Interaction Design class within the ISLT program. My group and I were tasked with redesigning an existing interface implementing interaction design principles to streamline the processes, cutdown on cognitive load, and just improve the user interaction altogether.

For this project, my team and I worked on redesigning the MU payment portal. This project stemmed from our own frustrations with how the website handled user interaction. This project focuses on the collaborative work and performance support aspects of Objective 1.1. Additionally, in this project I took on a project management role. Driving the team to stay organized, communicating overall goals and objectives, ensuring we were sticking to our timeline and meeting expected deadlines, and lastly delegating team responsibilities. Artifact 3 meets the objective of 1.3.

Artifact Reflection:

Overall this was an enjoyable project and really helped me to further capitalize on my professional strengths. In my day-to-day job, I am a project manager of sorts, helping to implement big changes or create new processes and procedures within the department. I feel I led the team well and we were able to meet the expectations and requirements for the assignment in time for every deadline. We used feedback throughout the course to help drive our design decisions and were only really limited by our inability to create a very high-end prototype.
