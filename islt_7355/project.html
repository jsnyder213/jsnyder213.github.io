2016 - Current: Operations Manager

My current job is the Operations Manager with the Shelden clinical simulation center at the University of Missouri. I have been with the simulation center for going on 6 years, starting at a simulation technican and advanceing myself into my current role as Operations manager. As the operations manager I am involved in the general day to day functionality of the center, this includes (but is not limited to)

Job Duties:

  • Project Management
  • Adminstration of a Learning Management system
  • Tech support

In this position I also manage a team of 3 people, as well as assit the director in the management of the department as a whole. Aside from the various responsibilities I have, that challenge me daily, one of the best aspects of this job is the technical work I do. I am responsibile for the upkeep and maintance of the center's high-fidelty simulators. These robots are used to help the center accomplish the mission of medical education in a safe and responbile learning environment.

Graphic Design

Although I have never done this "professionally" I am an artist and have ventured into digital art and graphic design throughout my years. Recently I did some minor graphic design work for a local micro brewery (BurOak) here in middle Missouri. This was a HUGE accomplishment for myself! As I stated, I have never done anything like this professionally but I have an artistc background and working on a project like this was an amazing experience.

For this project the brewery needed a label for beer that they had just developed, and at the time they had no artist on staff. I frequent the space to hangout and do art, eventually posting on social media. they really appreciated the artwork and my style and reached out for a possible collabration. I have a undergradute degree in Computer Animation and during that course work, i had done digital illustrations, but that had been many years ago. This project allowed me to revisit some of that knowledge and apply those long forgotten skills.

After completion of the label, I contuined to "work" with them on various artistic endevars such as art for their social media and a bandana design.
